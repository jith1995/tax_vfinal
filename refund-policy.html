Refund Policy

Refund policy for Your CA Partner ITR filing and CA-assisted tax support services.

MVP refund approach

Refund eligibility depends on whether filing work, CA review or consultation has started. If no work has begun, refund requests can be reviewed more easily.

Non-refundable situations

  • Completed consultation or CA review.
  • Work already started based on submitted documents.
  • Incorrect or incomplete information provided by the user.
  • Government portal delays or third-party issues beyond our control.

How to request a refund

Contact the team with your name, selected plan and payment reference. We will review the status of the work and respond with the applicable resolution.
